Rockin' Octopus

About Us

Rockin' Octopus is a Missoula based restaurant specializing in various Asian inspired dishes with a farm to table twist. We opened our doors in 2021 after running a successful food truck for two years. It is part of our mission to partner with Montana farms/ranches as well as vendors. We also hope to celebrate and honor the various Asian cultures our food is inspired by. Our menu will rotate seasonally with the ingredients from our wonderful partners. Thank you Missoula for allowing us to serve you and for helping us stay strong as a business.
