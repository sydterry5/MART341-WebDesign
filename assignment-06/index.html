Spicy Turkey Meatballs and Zoodles

<

<

<

Ingredients

    <
  • Ground turkey
    • <
  • Zucchini and sqaush noodles
    • <
  • Spaghetti Sauce
    • <
  • Finely chopped onion
    • <
  • Smoked paprika
    • <
  • Chili powder
    • <
  • Minced garlic
    • <
  • Bread crumbs
    • <
  • 1 egg yolk
    • <
  • Red pepper flakes
<

Directions

<
    <
  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees. Make meatballs by combing ground turkey, onion, breadcrumbs, garlic, egg, and spices. Form the meatballs by hand and evenly spread them out on frying pan. Brown sides of meatballs and bake in oven until middle reaches 165 degrees.
    2. <
  2. While meatballs are baking, pat dry noodles and oil a small skillet. Heat up sauce in saucepan then let simmer.
    3. <
  3. Toss noodles in skillet with salt, pepper, and garlic. Cook on medium heat for about 5 minutes, make sure noodles don't get too soft.
    4. <
  4. Once meatballs are cooked thoroughly, plate noodles, add sauce then top with two or three meatballs depending on size. Add parmesan cheese on top if you prefer.
